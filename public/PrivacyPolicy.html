Privacy Policy for Tennis Scorecard & Pickleball Scorecard

Effective Date: August 28, 2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy aims to provide information to users about how user data is handled within the Tennis Scorecard and Pickleball Scorecard apps (collectively referred to as "the apps"). Unless otherwise specified, all policies described in this document apply to both apps.

2. Data Collection, Storage, and Usage

a. Location Data: If granted permission from the user, the apps use location services to display the court location and weather in the match history. The location coordinates are transmitted to the Apple Weather Service to fetch the historical weather for the time and location of the match. The location data is processed and stored locally on device and/or in the user's iCloud account and is deleted if a match is deleted. This data is never transmitted to the app developer.

b. Photo Library Permissions: The apps request write-only access to the user's photo library solely for the purpose of allowing the user to save match screenshots to their Photos app. The apps do not request read access to the user's photo library and does not have access to any existing photos or other content in their library.

c. Health Data: The watchOS apps request permission to read and write health data to display workout activity metrics during a match and to save the workout to Apple Health. The health data accessed by the apps is processed and stored locally on device and/or in the user's iCloud account. This data is never transmitted to the app developer.

d. Other Data: The apps may store other data that is necessary for the proper functioning of the app, such as user-generated content, app-specific settings, and other related information. Any such data is processed and stored locally on device and/or in the user's iCloud account and is never transmitted to the app developer.

3. Third-Party Links

The apps contain links to third-party websites. These links are provided for the user's convenience and lead to websites that have their own privacy policies. Review the privacy policies of these third-party websites before providing any personal information or using their services.

4. No Sharing or Selling of Personal Data

The apps do not collect, access, or transmit to external entities any personally identifiable information. User data is not sold to third parties.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Updates to this Privacy Policy may be made periodically. Continued use of the apps signifies acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

6. Contact

Contact connectwithmichael@icloud.com by electronic mail with any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy.
